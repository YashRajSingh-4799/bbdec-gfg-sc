ABOUT US

Every college campus strives hard to create an atmosphere which promotes academic as well as good coding culture.

Geeks for Geeks student chapter of BBDEC, makes this come true. Our club focuses on enriching students' interest in coding and help them get better we organize webinars, Hackathons and a good competetive environment.

Our club conducts different activities for it's members round the year at the college and intercollegiate level . There are various events such as Hackathons, DSA and interview prepration tests on various topics and Training sessions for the club members. Our club also covers various major events of the college backed by GFG including Webinars by experts and plenty of other events.
